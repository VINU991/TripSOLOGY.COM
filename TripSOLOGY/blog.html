travel | Yashu gupta HIMACHAL

The Himachal Mountains, located in northern India, are a beautiful and unique range of mountains that offer stunning landscapes, diverse flora and fauna, and a rich cultural heritage.The Himachal Pradesh state, where the mountains are located, is known for its picturesque hill stations and charming villages, which attract tourists from all over the world. With its soaring peaks, deep valleys, and rolling hills, the Himachal Mountains are a popular destination for trekking, mountaineering, and skiing.

Nainital: A Scenic Hill Station in Uttarakhand. Nainital, also known as the Lake District of India, is a popular hill station located in the northern state of Uttarakhand. It is situated at an altitude of 2,084 meters above sea level and is surrounded by lush green hills and a serene lake that adds to its beauty. The lake, which is shaped like a pear, is the main attraction of the town and is known as Naini Lake
